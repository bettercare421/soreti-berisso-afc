Where Home Meets Exceptional Care

At Soreti Berisso, we believe every adult deserves to age with dignity in a beautiful, stress-free environment.

Located on a quiet cul-de-sac, our residence is a thoughtfully curated home that fosters independence, comfort, and peace of mind. We combine professional adult foster care standards with the genuine warmth of a family-centered home.

A Home Designed for Well-Being

Our home at 528 Park Pl offers a premium living experience with modern conveniences and safety-first design:

  • Accessible Luxury: Open-concept layout, Brazilian hardwood floors, and wide, accessible living spaces.
  • Year-Round Comfort: Radiant in-floor heating, central AC, and oversized windows with abundant natural light.
  • Relaxation & Nature: Maintenance-free deck, tranquil four-season porch, and nearby Vadnais Snail Lakes Regional Park.
  • Modern Security: Integrated smart-home security system supporting resident safety and peace of mind.
